# Newt

Newt is a lightweight service designed to integrate with Pangolin for enhanced home lab automation and monitoring. With Newt, you can easily connect your Runtipi instance to your Pangolin dashboard, enabling seamless management and visibility of your services.

## Features

- Integrates with Pangolin for centralized monitoring
- Simple configuration using environment variables
- Automatic service registration and status updates
- Lightweight and efficient resource usage

## How to Use

1. Install the Newt app from your custom app store in Runtipi.
2. Provide your Pangolin endpoint, Newt ID, and Newt secret during setup.
3. Start the service and monitor your home lab from the Pangolin dashboard.

## Requirements

- A running Pangolin instance
- Valid Newt credentials (ID and secret)
